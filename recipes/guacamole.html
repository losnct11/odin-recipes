Pasticho

Ingredients:

  • 5 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup minced green onion
  • 1/2 cup minced fresh cilantro
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Steps to follow:

  1. Stir together the avocado and lemon juice in a serving bowl; add the green onion and cilantro; mix well.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve immediately or store covered in refrigerator with avocado pits in the bowl to keep from browning.
